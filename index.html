About

Hello, my name is Kaden. I am a web developer currently residing in Vancouver, British Columbia. I am offering my web development and design skills to be able to study full-time to become a software engineer.

My goal is to develop a great understanding of many forms of programming, everywhere from blockchain programming to artificial intelligence. Ideally, my career focus would be directing production of artificial intelligence in a safe, ethical and positive direction - much like an idol of mine, Elon Musk.
